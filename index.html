What we do

Aloha! Your new mental health pal is finally here! Braini is a free, interactive mobile app for iOS and Android devices that allows you to keep track of all the factors that impact your everyday mental health. No matter how you are feeling, your personal, customized Braini knows how to help.

                                                                                                          About

                                               Why Braini is different

Braini allows you to track all 5 of the main factors that contribute to complete mental wellness! Braini understands that mental wellness involves more than just mindfulness. That’s why we are designed to track fitness, nutrition, hydration, and community engagement as well. Braini makes keeping track of these key factors easy and effective with revolutionized GPS tracking and Bluetooth interaction between users.

Introducing Braini rewards! By keeping track of your mental health, you can earn points towards discounts at your favorite local businesses! We’ve partnered up with Frost Kailua and Timmy T’s Gourmet Grinders to provide you with exciting discounts when you take control of your mental health.
